# Privacy Policy for Vent

**Effective date:** March 2, 2026 
**Last updated:** March 2, 2026

Vent ("we", "our", "us") provides a mobile application that allows users to create and view location-based posts. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address and authentication data
- Username and profile details you provide (such as display name and profile photo)

### Content You Create
- Posts, captions, comments, and related metadata
- Photos/videos you choose to upload
- Messages or reports you submit through the app

### Location Information
- Approximate or precise location (when you grant permission) to place and display posts on the map

### Device and Usage Information
- Device type, operating system, app version
- Basic diagnostic and error logs for reliability and troubleshooting

## 2. Permissions We Request

Vent may request these permissions:
- **Location**: to show nearby posts and allow location-based posting
- **Camera**: to capture photos/videos for posts
- **Photos/Media**: to upload media from your device
- **Microphone**: when recording media content (if used)

You can disable permissions in your device settings, but some features may not work.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use your data to:
- Provide core app features (account, posts, map, social interactions)
- Personalize your experience
- Maintain security, prevent abuse, and enforce policies
- Debug, monitor, and improve app performance

We do not sell personal information.
